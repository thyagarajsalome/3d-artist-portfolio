About Me

Turning Concepts Into
3D Reality

With over 10+ years of experience in 3D design, I specialise in architectural visualisation, interior rendering, product design, and event theming — delivering high-fidelity visuals that communicate the full potential of a space before a single brick is laid.

I work with a powerful toolkit that includes 3ds Max, V-Ray, SketchUp, AutoCAD, and Photoshop, combining technical precision with an eye for drama, light, and material detail.

Whether it's a luxury residential interior, a commercial showroom, or a themed event stage — I translate client briefs into visuals that sell the vision.
